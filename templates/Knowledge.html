非物质文化遗产知识图谱

非物质文化遗产知识图谱是非物质文化遗产保护和传承的一种重要工具。它通过将非物质文化遗产的相关信息进行系统化、结构化的整合，形成一种图谱形式的知识体系，旨在更好地保护和传承这些珍贵的文化遗产。 The knowledge graph of intangible cultural heritage serves as a vital tool for the protection and transmission of intangible cultural heritage. By systematically and structurally integrating relevant information about intangible cultural heritage, it forms a graphical knowledge system aimed at better safeguarding and transmitting these valuable cultural legacies.
